Whoops! is a story you can take part in about making mistakes on the internet. Or you could call it a "public shaming simulator"

It's about what happens when we act poorly, say or do occasional stupid things that used to be in front of a handful of people we now broadcast to potential audiences of millions. Are our cultural tools regulating public behaviour fit for the internet age? Do we as a society have the right tools to manage debate about controversial topics online?

You'll get a 90 minute slot at a random time in the next week to receive the narrative via text, phone and email. It doesn't require you to do anything or to dedicate any time and no acting or roleplay is required - you just receive. You can stop at any time by replying STOP to any SMS you receive. You can do this from anywhere in the world.

Content warning: as part of the experience you will receive some nasty texts with some offensive language.
